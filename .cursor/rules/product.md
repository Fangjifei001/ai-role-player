# Product Context

## Product Name
AI Role Player - Voice-Based Customer Sales Skills

## Product Goal
Build a working full-stack demo that helps sales users practice realistic customer conversations through voice-first role play, with configurable scenarios/personas and post-session coaching feedback.

## Primary Business Outcomes
- Improve training effectiveness with realistic, repeatable conversations.
- Enable configurability through admin-managed scenarios and personas.
- Maintain low perceived latency for natural conversation flow.

## User Roles
- Learner (sales trainee): starts and runs role-play sessions, reviews feedback.
- Admin: creates and manages scenarios/personas and related scoring/behavior config.

## Mandatory Product Areas
1. Learner Dashboard (start flow)
2. Role Play Session (voice-first conversation)
3. Coaching & Feedback Summary
4. Admin Console (scenario/persona configuration)

## Product Priorities
1. Voice UX quality and responsiveness over visual polish.
2. Acceptance criteria completeness over extra features.
3. Simple, maintainable architecture over premature optimization.
4. Demo reliability: robust handling for common failures and edge cases.
